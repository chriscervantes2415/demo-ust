The University Traditions

Freshmen Orientation

Embark on your journey at UST Legazpi with our engaging Freshmen Orientation, designed to introduce you to campus life, academic resources, and fellow students.

The Thomasian Welcome Walk

Join us in this symbolic tradition as we welcome new students into the Thomasian family with a celebratory walk around campus, fostering unity and camaraderie.

Paskuhan (Christmas Celebration)

Experience the magic of the holiday season with our annual Paskuhan celebration, filled with festive decorations, performances, and joyous festivities.

The Baccalaureate Mass

Commemorate the achievements of our graduating students with a solemn Baccalaureate Mass, offering blessings for their future endeavors.

Retreats and Recollections

Take a moment for reflection and spiritual growth through our retreats and recollections, offering opportunities for personal development and renewal.

We are passionate education dedicated to providing high-quality resources learners all backgrounds.
